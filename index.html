What I offer

Web Design

Designing websites based on your needs that work well both on desktop and mobile, a responsive and adaptive design. In responsive design, content moves dynamically depending on screen size; in adaptive design, the website content is fixed in layout sizes that match common screen sizes.

Online Store

Customize your Online Store theme for PrestaShop, Woo Commerce by WordPress or your Shopify store.
Create an online store with a perfect look and feel for your brand. A professional and attractive website that meets the needs of your customers.

Landing Page

A standalone web page for a marketing or advertising campaign. It’s where a visitor “lands” after they click on a link in an email, or ads from Google, YouTube, Facebook,etc. Landing page is the best option for increasing the conversion rates of your marketing campaigns and lowering your cost of acquiring a lead or sale.

Portfolio Site

Create a beautiful & trusting page that match your brand as a photographer, an artist, a professional such architect, doctor, lawyer, etc.

The website for your prefesional brand identity can be crafted nicely according to your need and target market.

WordPress CMS

I design Wordpress CMS customized websites. Help you improve your current WordPress website or start with a new development project customized. WordPress allows you to have a blog, sell products, and modify everything in a very simple way everything you need.

Company Profile

It could be a simple page or website that shows every service that your company has to offer.

What included

Simple & Easy Navigation

Site visitors are always in a hurry. That's why User Experience (UX) plays a key role in helping visitors use, understand and stay on your website. Having an obvious, logical navigation with clear hierarchy & use consistent layouts and visual cues for functionality across the site is a must.

SEO Friendly

A nice looking website that’s easy to use, needs to earn traffic. Otherwise, all that effort in design, UX and content development will be useless. Your site will also follow rules and guidelines for effective search engine optimization.

Responsive View

Your site must look great and work well on any platform. The growth of mobile and tablet devices is getting higher and that might be what your next visitor will be using. Optimizing for mobile will improve both the experience of your visitors and your SEO Rankings.

Fast Loading

A fast loading page is great both for user and search engine. This is one of the most important requirement because user's attention span is getting shorter and a fast loading page can get a higher rank by search engine.

User Friendly

Internet users have notoriously short attention spans and even less patience for slow, complicated tasks. That's why a simple site is easier to use for most people and they like it better than a fancy looking site which brings more complication and headache.
